Persuading a Shift to Healthier Eating Habits

Computer Science Undergraduate Research Assistant Summer Job

Image taken from Wikicommons

I worked with a PhD student on her research focused on persuasion technology and healthier eating. The overarching goal for this project was to examine why people eat healthy and use this information to persuade others to make healthier eating choices.
Team
Prof. Julita Vassileva, Ifeoma Adaji, Simone Debrowney, and Czarina Catambing Sharmaine
Link to published article

The project was divided into a survey examining people's healthy eating habits and an analysis of users' personalities from reviews posted on pulse recipes from an online recipe community, Allrecipes.com. The data was extracted from the website using a web crawler and scraper that was built using a combination of Python, Beautiful Soup, Selenium, Webdriver, and SQLite.
