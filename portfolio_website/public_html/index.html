About Me


Resume

I recently graduated from the University of Saskatchewan where I completed my Bachelor of Arts and Science, majoring in Interactive Systems Design. I became intrigued when I first learned about human computer interaction; a field of study that is focused on design and examining how users interact with technology. I am most interested in user interface design, user experience design, and usability testing. In my spare time, I enjoy participating in roleplaying games, gardening, and studying Japanese.
